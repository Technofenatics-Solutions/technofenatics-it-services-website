Company History

Technofenatics was born out of a vision to provide comprehensive digital solutions to businesses across the globe. Our journey began in January 2020, when we started offering our services to clients. Though we operated informally at first, our commitment to quality and client satisfaction led to rapid growth. In December 2023, we officially registered as a company, marking a significant milestone in our evolution.

In just a few short years, we've successfully completed over 30 projects for clients spanning multiple countries, including Australia, Singapore, India (Mumbai, Pune, Kanpur, Delhi), and beyond. Our international client base is a testament to the quality and reliability of our services.
